Asset Registry
Track IT equipment, vehicles, tools, furniture, and any physical asset. Custom fields, categories, sub-categories, parent-child relationships, and bulk imports from Excel.
🔄
Check-in / Check-out
Full checkout lifecycle with due dates, reservation calendar, overdue alerts, and return history. Know exactly who has what and when it's coming back.
🔧
Maintenance Management
Schedule, assign, and track maintenance tasks with priority levels. Full service history, cost tracking, and automatic overdue detection.
📜
Contracts & Licenses
Store contracts, software licenses, warranties, and insurance policies in one place. Automatic expiry alerts so you never miss a renewal.
📷
Barcode & QR Scanning
Three scan modes: Quick Lookup, Audit Mode for full inventory verification with CSV export, and Field Update to edit assets on the spot without going back to a desk.
🤖
AI Insights Engine
Predictive failure detection, cost-saving recommendations, utilization scoring, and anomaly alerts — generated automatically from your real data.
📊
Advanced Analytics
Live charts, depreciation calculators, fleet health scores, utilization heatmaps, and 6+ exportable reports. Real data, not static screenshots.
📸
Asset Photo Management
Capture photos directly with your camera or upload from your device. Photos attach to assets and appear in your registry, reports, and audit exports.
🏷️
Consumables Inventory
Track consumable items like office supplies, spare parts, and materials separately from fixed assets. Monitor stock levels and set low-stock alerts.
